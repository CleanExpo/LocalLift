Getting Started

Welcome to LocalLift

LocalLift is a comprehensive platform designed to help local businesses improve their online presence and customer engagement.

This guide will help you navigate the platform and get the most out of its features. Let's start with the basics:

Step 1: Set up your business profile

Complete your business profile with accurate information about your business, including contact details, business hours, and a description of your services.

Step 2: Explore the dashboard

Familiarize yourself with the dashboard, which provides an overview of your business performance, achievements, and reporting tools.

Step 3: Set goals and track progress

Define your business goals and use the achievement system to track your progress towards these goals.

Business Analytics

Understanding Your Analytics

LocalLift provides comprehensive analytics to help you understand your business performance and customer engagement. Here's what you need to know:

Performance Metrics

Track key performance indicators like customer visits, engagement rate, and conversion metrics.

Engagement Statistics

Understand how customers interact with your business online, including social media engagement and review activity.

Trend Analysis

View trends over time to identify patterns and opportunities for improvement.

Using Analytics Effectively

To get the most out of your analytics:

  1. Regularly review your performance metrics to identify trends.
  2. Compare current performance to historical data to measure growth.
  3. Identify areas of strength and opportunities for improvement.
  4. Use insights to inform your business decisions and marketing strategies.
  5. Set measurable goals based on your analytics data.
